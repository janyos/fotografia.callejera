This is a celebration that is celebrated in Santa Cruz every May 3, in which many people from different places and cultures come either to make a pilgrimage, to pray, or simply to have fun and taste the different sweets and breads that are sold. how we can see it in the image, different dances are also performed as an offering and veneration to the lord of the hill, all live together and have fun ignoring any difference between them.

Thanks to this type of event, you can also get to know different types of cultures, and enjoy each one of them, each one has its own unique and unrepeatable style.
